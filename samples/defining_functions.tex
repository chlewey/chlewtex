  \funcdef fx{\mathbf R}*[x^2]

  \funcdef{\textrm{exp}}x{\mathbf R}[e^x]

  \funcdef\exp x{\mathbf R}[e^x][\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\left(1+\frac xn\right)^n]

  \funcdef{\textrm{sqrt}}n[\mathbf N]{\mathbf R}
\]

Function with different domain and codomain, and alternative writing: \[
  \funcdef{\textrm{sqrt}}n[\mathbf N]{\mathbf R}[\sqrt n]
\]

Function with different domain and codomain, alternative writing and declaration: \[
  \funcdef{\textrm{sqrt}}n[\mathbf N]{\mathbf R}[\sqrt n][\exp(\frac12\ln n)]
